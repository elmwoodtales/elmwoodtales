About the Author & Illustrator

K. Elmwood is a author and illustrator creating historical and fantasy stories infused with feminine power and rage, progressive beliefs, and strong touches of romance. She is currently editing her first novel, writing a second, and playing about with shorter works of fiction while figuring out her next steps.

Away from creating new worlds she loves to read as many books as she can, take long walks in forests, and listen to too much Florence + the Machine.

She lives in Belfast with her partner, daughter and three very different cats.

Atomic

My main focus at the moment, a novel where science and magic are one and the same.

After years away from her homeland, a pharmacist is wrenched back by a horrific event. Finding herself surrounded by injustice and rebellion, she must face up to the reasons she fleed to another shore in the first place, and discover if she has it in her to change to a cruel world.

Displaced

My first novel, a secondary world historical romance, currently in the final stages of editing.

Eight years after her exile, a woman has built a life she adores in a nation where she is the equal of any man. But when her secret past catches up with her, it threatens to bring everything to ruin.

A prince stands on the cusp of a new life, one planned for him since birth. But all he feels is the last thread of freedom slipping through his fingers. When he’s betrayed by the one he loves deepest, his cracks begin to show.
